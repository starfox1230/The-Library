    About this pocket guide

    This app is a compact rendering of March 2023 body-procedure and lung-biopsy laboratory and antithrombotic guidance. It keeps the lung-biopsy pathway separate because its recommendations differ from the general low- and high-risk body pathways.

    Values exactly at a written boundary are treated conservatively. Missing values are shown as items to verify, not assumed normal. Individual management remains at the physician’s discretion.
