Relative Intensity- The dB. A change of 10 in the dB scale corresponds to two orders of
magnitude (100 times) and so forth. 1 he dB is based on a log 10 scale.
•
Reducing the sound intensity to 10% is -10 dB
•
Reducing to 1% is - 20 dB
•
Reducing to 0.1% is- 30 dB
Testable Point: A loss of 3 dB (-3 dB) represents a 50% loss of signal intensity (power).
Testable Point: The tissue thickness that reduces the ultrasound intensity by 3 dB is
considered the "half-value" thickness.
