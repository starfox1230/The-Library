Effect of Ionizing Radiation of Blood:
Lymphocytes are the MOST sensitive blood cells in the body. A dose of 0.25 Gy is enough to
depress the amount circulating in the blood.
Lymphocytes can actually be stimulated to divide and you can score the number of chromosomal
aberrations at first mitosis to reflect average total body dose.
