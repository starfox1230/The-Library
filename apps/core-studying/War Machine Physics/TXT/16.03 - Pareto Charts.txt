Pareto Charts
This is a chart named after Vifredo Pareto, who accomplished many great things in his life
including popularizing the term “ 01116” . marrying a women 30 years his junior (WIN!), and
collecting a large number of cats- which he famously treated better than his house guests (he fed
the cats first, and made his dinner guests wait). Oh, he also made a chart.
The chart It’s essentially a prioritization exercise. 1 got 99 problems, which l (s) is the major
contributor. You take causes to a problem, and rank them in the order that they contribute to that
problem. The problems sum together to 100% of the problem (bars are ranked in descending
order, creating a concave function). The point is to show how 2 or 3 things can cause 80% of the
issue. The entire purpose of the chart is to highlight the most important factor among a set
of factors. The idea is that fixing the main cause, may fix the problem.
