What is this “ Electromagnetic Radiation?”
This is basically a wave of energy that does not
require a media to travel in (it can travel in a vacuum). Its velocity is fixed at 3 x 108 m/s
Remember these from general chemistry?
Velocity = Frequency x Wavelength
Energy = Frequency x some constant “ h”
Because the velocity is fixed , frequency and wavelength have an inverse relationship. As one
goes up, the other goes down. As these factors change, the quality of the wave changes.
Depending on the frequency and wavelength - an electromagnetic wave has different
qualities. For example, long wavelengths (low frequency) can carry radiant heat. Whereas,
shorter wavelengths (high frequency) can carry radio, tv, and radar signals.

WTFdoes “ Ionizing " even mean ? As the frequency increases the wave gets more energetic and
will have the ability to remove electrons from an atom. This is called “ ionizing radiation.”
Since it takes 15eV of energy to remove an electron from an atom, a photon with energy
greater than 15ev would be ionizing.
