Deterministic (Non-Stochastic) - “ Cause and effect.” There is a threshold. Once you cross the
threshold you get the result (burn, cataract, gut necrosis etc..). After you cross this threshold the more
dose you get the more severe that thing gets.
Stochastic - “ Shit happens” Stochastic Eiffects are Chance Liascd - and follow a Linear Quadratic No
Threshold model - where risk increases with dose but doesn't automatically result in a bad outcome.
