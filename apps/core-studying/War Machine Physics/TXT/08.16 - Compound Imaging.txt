This vs That - Harmonics vs Compound Imaging
These toys are great, but sometimes they cause problems. The classic way to
illustrate this is to think about a breast “nodule” in mammo.
Normal

totally anechoic

The bottom line is Harmonics can make a solid thing looks cystic (by turning it
anechoic), and Compound Imaging can make a cystic thing look solid (by
removing the posterior features).
