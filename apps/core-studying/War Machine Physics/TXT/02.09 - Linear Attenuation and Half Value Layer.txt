Linear Attenuation and Half Value Layer
People will sometimes describe the relationship between these two things. It’s fairly
intuitive but I'll walk you through it real fast.
Scenario 1: You have a high energy photon
• The photon has lots of energy and will penetrate
further.
• Further Penetration means you have “ less
attenuation"- remember lower attenuation at
higher kVP.
• The half value layer - which is a term for the
amount of material required to attenuate an x-ray
to 1/2 the original output, will need to be
increased.
