Electronic Magnification (Zoom) — If you decrease the input field ot view by half, then
only one fourth of the input phosphor will be irradiated. If all other parameters are held
constant, the brightness will also drop to one quarter. This gets the attention of the
automatic exposure control which will ramp up the juice (on average 1.4 - 2.Ox dose per
setting increase).
