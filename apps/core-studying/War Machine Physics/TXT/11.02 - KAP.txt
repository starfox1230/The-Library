KAP Source Distance Independent
KAP - This is best thought of as “ the whole beam.'’ The highest yield most testable point is
that KAP is totally independent of the source distance. You can measure it at any point
along its path and it will be the same.
How can that he? As you get further and further away from the source the dose decreases via
the inverse square law BUT the distance increases via divergence also by the square. The two
cancel out.
Practical / Testable Point of this source independence? Geometric Magnification. If you bring
the patient closer to the source you will increase the magnification. The testable point is that
geometric magnification will increase Entrance Skin KERMA (greater risk of a burn), but
NOT change KAP.
