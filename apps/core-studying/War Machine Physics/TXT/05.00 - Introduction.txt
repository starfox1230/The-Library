Gamesmanship - When you arc studying mammo for physics I want you to concentrate on
what makes mammo unique - relative to regular plain old diagnostic x-ray. Remember by
learning via comparison you arc training yourself to exclude distractors - a key tactic for
multiple choice exams.
Overview — Why does mammo need to he different? The difference in attenuation of a
breast cancer and just regular breast tissue is very small. So you have to use lower energy and
a nearly mono-energetic beam to enhance the attenuation differences. Additionally, an
increase in spatial resolution is required to see micro calcifications.
