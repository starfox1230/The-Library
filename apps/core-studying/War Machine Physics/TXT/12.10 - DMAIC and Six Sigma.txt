Making People Miserable - DMAIC Methodology
This is very similar to the PDSA method - which essentially follows a common sense approach.
But instead of just saying that, the QA officer tries to act like they invented something by calling
it another vocabulary word.
Define -> Measure -> Analyze -> Improve -> Control
In other words, state the problem, find a way to quantify or measure the problem, guess where
the problems are, make an intervention, and then see if it worked. If they work- they act like a
control to keep you from sliding back into your pattern of screwing up.
Trivia: DMAIC is “ Data Driven”
Trivia: DMAIC is the Primary Tool for Six Sigma Projects (although DMAIC is not exclusive to
Six Sigma), it can be used for any random project.
WTF is Six Sigma ?
Six Sigma is a process to identify and remove
trouble makers and minimize variability. The
name “ Six Sigma” describes the standard
deviation of defect-free products it creates -
in this case 6. What that actually means is
that 99.9999998% of all opportunities to
produce the product are expected to be free of
defects (3.4 defective features per million
opportunities). This seems like a reasonable
goal for a Radiology resident to be held
accountable too.
