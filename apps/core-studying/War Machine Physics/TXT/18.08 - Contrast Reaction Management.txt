Why is the concentration for IV Epi different than SubQ?
The way to think about this is that you need a large volume of fluid for IV - beeause you
are going to push it around. Alternatively, you wouldn't want a large volume of fluid
under the skin. The dose is actually the same (0.1 - 0.3mg) it's just the dilution volume
that is different.
SC or IM: 1:1,000, 0.1 to 0.3 ml (0.1 to 0.3 mg)
IV: 1:10,000 , 1 to 3 ml ( 0.1 to 0.3 mg)
