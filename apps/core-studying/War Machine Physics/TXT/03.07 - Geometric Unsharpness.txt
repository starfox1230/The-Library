Geometric Relationship - Influences Performance:
Geometric Unsharpness: This occurs because of several factors:
•
Focal Spot: Small Focal Spot = Less Blur
•
Source-Object Distance: Closer the source is to the image = More Blur
•
Object Detector Distance: Closer the object is to the detector = Less Blur
•
Magnification: More Magnification = More Blur
Below is a diagram showing how a change in focal spot will change the size of geometric
unsharpness. As you can see, a smaller focal spot has less “ unsharpness.”
