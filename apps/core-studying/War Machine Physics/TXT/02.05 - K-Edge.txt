The “ K-Edge” - A Game of Shadows
I want to start by defining a term that we throw around a lot - but often don’t think about
what it actually means. “ Contrast” What is this “Contrast" ? Contrast is the ability to
tell that two things standing next to each other are different. The more contrast - the
easier it is to tell them apart. For example, the white lion and the black lion - standing
next to each other - the difference is obvious. Even if they were very small you’d be
able to tell them apart. There is a high “contrast” between white and black. Now
compare that to the Black Lion vs the Dark Grey Lion. Yes you can tell them apart - but
it’s not as easy. There is less contrast between these two similar shades.

contrast. What you get is a white thing (contrast) next to darker things.
