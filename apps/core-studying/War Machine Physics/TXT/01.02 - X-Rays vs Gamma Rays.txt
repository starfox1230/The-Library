This vs That: X-Rays vs Gamma Rays
Both are used for medical imaging, and both are ionizing - the difference lies in their
origin stories.
Gamma Rays - This type of radiation turned Bruce Banner into the Incredible Hulk.
Aside from creating giant green rage monsters, it's also the primary tool of the nuclear
medicine imager. These rays originate from the nuclei of an atom. They are excess
energy given off as an atom decays in an attempt to become more stable. More on this in
the Nukes chapter.
X-Rays - This type of radiation allows Superman to see what color Lois Lane’s
underwear is (as long as she’s not standing behind a lead flower pot). X-Rays originate
from interactions between fast moving electrons and atoms.
