“ Never Event”
• As the name infers this is something that should “ never happen”.
• Testable Trivia = This vocabulary word was invented by the National Quality forum (NGR
“ Sentinel Event”
• Serious Injury / Death OR a close call.
• Trivia- this always triggers an investigation
• Trivia- 15 Gray exposure is a sentinel event
• Classic Example- Wrong patient, wrong site, wrong procedure.
Adverse Event:
• Injury Cause by medical Care.
• Trivia- It does NOT have to be someone’s fault. It does NOT mean an error occurred
Example- Guy with horrible emphysema needs a lung biopsy. The procedure is done with
“ perfect” technical by the Attending while the Trainee watches in awe from the corner of the
room. The patient develops a small pneumothorax- which is promptly treated with a chest tube.
The development of a pneumo in this case is an “ Adverse Event” - but it happen because of
unfavorable conditions / bad luck not because of an error.
