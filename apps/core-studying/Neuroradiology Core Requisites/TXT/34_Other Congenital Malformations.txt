                                                                           CONGENITAL HYDROCEPHALUS

                                                                            Among the congenital malformations of the brain,
                                                                              Chiari II malformation and Dandy-Walker malformation
                                                                              are the most common causes of hydrocephalus.

                                                                            Chiari II malformation accounts for 40% of congenital
                                                                              hydrocephalus in children.

                                                                            Seventy to eighty percent of children with Dandy-Walker
                                                                              malformation have hydrocephalus.
