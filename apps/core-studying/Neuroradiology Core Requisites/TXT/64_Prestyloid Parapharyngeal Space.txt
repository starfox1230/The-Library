                                                                         Prestyloid Parapharyngeal Space

                                                                          In the prestyloid parapharyngeal space, primary lesions
                                                                            are extremely uncommon, given the limited tissue types
                                                                            in this location (fat, ectopic minor salivary gland tissue,
                                                                            and lymph nodes). The most common intrinsic lesion
                                                                            involves enlarged lymph nodes, either inflammatory
                                                                            or neoplastic, followed by minor salivary gland tumors
                                                                            (with pleomorphic adenomas being the most common),
                                                                            and lipomas.
